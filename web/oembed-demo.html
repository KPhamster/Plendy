üìã What is Plendy?

Plendy is a location-based experience sharing app that helps users save, organize, and share places they discover on social media. When users share Instagram or Facebook posts containing restaurant recommendations, travel spots, or other locations, Plendy uses Meta's oEmbed API to display rich previews of this content.

How oEmbed is Used:

  • ‚úì Preview Instagram Posts: When a user shares an Instagram post URL, we use oEmbed to fetch and display the post preview with caption and media.
  • ‚úì Preview Facebook Content: Facebook page posts and videos are embedded using oEmbed to show rich previews.
  • ‚úì Extract Location Data: The caption text from oEmbed response helps us identify location mentions for saving experiences.
